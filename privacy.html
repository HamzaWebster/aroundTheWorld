Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Our Mission

Around the World is a private digital journal curated exclusively by our team of authorized explorers. This platform is designed to showcase the authentic travel experiences, photography, and audio-visual recordings of our site owners.

Content Ownership

All content published on this website—including but not limited to high-resolution photography, cinematic videos, ambient audio recordings, and descriptive narratives—is the exclusive intellectual property of the site owners. Visitors are encouraged to explore and enjoy these materials for personal inspiration, but unauthorized reproduction or distribution is strictly prohibited.

User Roles

To maintain the integrity and personal nature of this journal, content publication is restricted to the established project contributors. General visitors do not have the ability to create accounts, upload media, or publish stories. This ensures a consistent, premium experience curated by those who have lived these adventures.

Data Collection

As a viewer-only platform, we do not collect personal data from our visitors. We do not use cookies for tracking, nor do we require any registration. Your journey through our stories is entirely private and anonymous.

Contact

If you have questions regarding our curation process or the stories shared here, please reach out through our team's established contact channels.
